Caitlin Westerfield

Hi, my name is Caitlin and I am a recent 2020 graduate of Yale University originally from Evanston, IL. I am particularly interested in using technology to address equity gaps within the education system and in developing policy to prevent human rights infringements by technology companies.

After graduating in May 2020, I will be working at the Chan Zuckerburg Initiative on the Education Initiative's Learning Plaform Team. Prior to starting work in January 2021, I am working for the Sara Gideon for Maine Senate Campaign.

Please feel free to send me an email at caitlin.westerfield@yale.edu. I would love to hear from you, to talk about my experiences!
